About Us

We are Dealers for the three main brands in off road competition today – Suzuki, Yamaha and Honda Motocross Machinery, all available with a reliable parts backup in place as well. Moreover, we race. This means we understand the demands of the rider and racer and we are able to provide not only fantastic products and services, but real world advice on any motocross related problems you may be facing. If its happening to you, it has happened to one of us.. From a Grand Prix Rider and mechanic to club level weekend warrior we have a wealth of experience available to help get you up and racing! Throughout the years brands have come and gone along with trends and fashions making it ever more vital to have in supply the best equipment available on the market. Therefore anyone who requires anything relating to off road competition should look no further than Russells Motorcycles We are official dealers of Honda, Yamaha and Suzuki motorcycles and have a full stock of genuine parts and accesories to ensure the quality of customer care continues into after-sales. As well as stocking what we regard to be the 3 most prominent brands of machinery in the industry, we also deal in the latest high quality clothing, accessories and protection on the market. Knowing that nowadays style and fit are every bit as vital as safety when choosing riding gear we have selected specific brands to stock which not only exceed safety regulations but are cutting edge in design as well. These include Alpinestars boots and clothing, Answer and Fly race wear, Berik boots, Oakley Progrip and Smith goggles, Arai, Lazer and One Industries helmets, SixSixOne and EVS armour and protection and the Leatt neck Brace. As well as the best quality products available for the rider, we also pride ourselves on being able to supply the industries’ leading brands when it comes to after market perishable and performance parts for your bike. Brands like Akrapovic Leo Vince Pro Circuit and FMF exhausts, Renthal sprockets, D.I.D. and Regina chains, Polisport and UFO plastics, One Industries N-Style and FLU graphics, Pro Carbon protection, Castrol Silkolene and Q8 oils and lubricants, Twin Air filters, Talon/Excel wheels, Michelin Dunlop and Bridgestone tyres and Pro Taper bars & Grips. This list is only a small reflection of our product base, But provides an insight into the quality of our stock.
